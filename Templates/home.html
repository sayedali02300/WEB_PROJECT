Welcome to LearnTech

LearnTech is a platform created to assist beginners who find it hard to learn how to build a website from scratch. The goal of LearnTech is to provide a strong base in web development by teaching the main concepts step by step. This starts with understanding the basics of HTML and CSS and leads to creating fully functional, responsive websites. The platform emphasizes practical and accessible learning, offering users hands-on experience and real examples to build their skills and confidence in coding.
